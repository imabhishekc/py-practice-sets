Formula 1 (F1) racing is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing. 
It features cutting-edge technology, elite drivers, and races held on tracks across the world. 
Each season, teams compete in Grand Prix events to earn points toward the World Championship. 
Speed, strategy, and innovation play crucial roles in every race. 
F1 is known for its thrilling overtakes, high-speed corners, and passionate fanbase.
